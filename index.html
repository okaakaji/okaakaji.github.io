February 19: Out of This World

Roses are Red

Violets are blue

You are as pretty as an aesthetic view

But violets are indeed purple

and that is your favorite loon

Beautiful and smart you got it all, nothing left to hone

Gorgeous, mesmerising, and breathtaking

are some words that come to mind

Everytime I see you, your eyes sweep me into a world so divine

I promised you tonight, I will give you the perfect date

my only intention is to cherish you and make you feel elate

Dont overthink dont concentrate

let the rest of the night be written by fate

tes lèvres sont aussi douces que des fruits

j'ai une intention avec toi ma chère

c'est de vous chérir tous les jours et de vous faire sentir jolie
